Every evening, bakeries and stores have surplus food. We built a simple system to get it to families who need it — before midnight.

How it works
1
Register via WhatsApp
Send us a message with your name and family size. We send back your personal QR card immediately.
2
Visit a store before closing
Participating bakeries leave packed portions outside the door every evening before they close.
3
Scan and collect — no one watching
Open your QR from WhatsApp, point your camera, collect your packets. No queues, no questions.
"No one should have to ask a stranger for food. A scan is enough."
— The idea behind this system
Built on the belief that technology should serve people quietly —
not make them prove their need.
